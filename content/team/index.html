Cecilia Corral

Vice President of Product
Co-Founder

Cecilia oversees all aspects of product development, including the core CareMessage application and associated disease management content. She works with partner organizations and patients to design, test and refine CareMessage products. She is proficient in the process of human-centered design pioneered by the Hasso Plattner Institute of Design (d.school) at Stanford University, where she graduated with a degree in Product Design Engineering. A native Spanish speaker who grew up in South Texas and Mexico, Cecilia helps ensure that all CareMessage programs accommodate the cultural and linguistic needs of our minority patients.

Manuel Rivera, MS

Director of Health Informatics
Co-Founder

Manuel Rivera is a software engineer who leads all aspects of privacy, security and interoperability for CareMessage's core product. He comes to the organization with a passion for health IT and expertise with real-time client-server communication, computer graphics, and interaction design. Manuel has an MS in Telecommunication Engineering from the Technical School of Madrid (UPM) with a specialty in Biomedical Engineering and has studied the social impact of technology at UC Berkeley.

Daniel Naves

Engineering Manager

Daniel is a full stack software engineer with over 10 years of experience, and has expertise in Ruby on Rails, Javascript, HTML, CSS, Git, SASS, and HAML. He has previously built multiple web and mobile platforms for both startups and Fortune 500 companies, and loves working on important projects that can have a significant impact.

Patricia Sanchez, MPH

Director of Content

Patricia helps develop many of CareMessage's programs, especially those focused on childhood and adolescence health issues. She comes to the organization with a passion to improve health literacy among low income populations. She has over 8 years of experience creating health education materials that are both culturally and low literacy appropriate. She has worked with a Medi-Cal managed care population at the Contra Costa Health Plan, and holds a Masters in Public Health (MPH) from San Diego State University.

Karen Finch, MD

Health Content Specialist, Mental Health

Greg Burrell, MD

Director of Research and Clinical Solutions

Greg leads the clinical review and oversight of CareMessage's programs and works with the Scientific Advisory Board to produce high-quality data to help drive informed decisions about CareMessage's clinical products. He comes to the organization with a passion to educate and empower patients to make smarter healthcare decisions, as well as creating and implementing new technologies to improve healthcare outcomes. Greg is currently an Assistant Professor of Clinical Medicine at the University of California, San Francisco (UCSF). He received his bachelors degree from Northwestern, his medical degree from the Pritzker School of Medicine at the University of Chicago, and completed his internal medicine residency at UCSF.
