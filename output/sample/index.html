SugarTalk and PláticaDulce

SugarTalk and PláticaDulce are year long programs that comprise shorter 4-6 week long modules addressing topics most relevant to type 2 diabetics — such as properly injecting insulin, recognizing a high or low blood glucose or avoiding culturally-specific foods that tend to increase blood glucose. The programs are tailored to various factors, including disease progression, age, gender, ethnicity and cultural preferences.

Improve Patient Attitude

Address cultural barriers to accepting type 2 diabetes as a diagnosis, and help patients self-actualize and understand factors that motivate them to change.

Simplify Medical Terms

Enable patients to understand the relationship between glucose, insulin and cholesterol.

Test Results

Help patients understand the significance of their test results, such as HbA1C and cholesterol, and remind them when they're due for their next checkup.

Diabetes Self-Care

Teach patients to self-manage various aspects of their condition, such as conducting regular foot exams at home.

Blood Glucose Reminders

Help patients remember to measure fasting blood glucose regularly, and enable them to track their blood glucose levels via text messaging.

Flexible Engagement

Patients can be enrolled in the entire year long program, or any of the shorter 4-6 week long modules.
