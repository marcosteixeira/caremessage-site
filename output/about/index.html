The Team

We are Engineers. Physicians. Designers. Scientists. United under the mission of bringing mobile health to the underserved.

Vineet Singal

Chief Executive Officer
Co-Founder

Vineet helps set the strategic direction of the organization. Previously, he led a team at Stanford Business School that helped register over 115,000 people into the bone marrow registry using social media. He has also conducted and published peer-reviewed research in cancer biology, opthalmology, neurology and epidemiology at the Mount Sinai School of Medicine, the National Institutes of Health and the Stanford School of Medicine. A Forbes ’30 under 30’ social entrepreneur, Vineet has served as a student representative on the Stanford Board of Trustees and graduated with University Distinction from Stanford.

Manuel Rivera, MS

Chief Product Officer
Co-Founder

Manuel Rivera is a software engineer who leads all aspects of CareMessage's product development. He comes to the organization with a passion for health IT and expertise with real-time client-server communication, computer graphics, and interaction design. Manuel has an MS in Telecommunication Engineering from the Technical School of Madrid (UPM) with a specialty in Biomedical Engineering and has studied the social impact of technology at UC Berkeley.

Cecilia Corral

Director
Product Management

Cecilia leads program development efforts for CareMessage, and works with our partners to test and refine our products. She is proficient in the process of user-centered design pioneered by the Hasso Plattner Institute of Design (d.school) at Stanford University and graduated from Stanford with a degree in Product Design under the guidance of David Kelley, the Founder of IDEO and Stanford d.school. A native Spanish speaker who grew up in Mexico, Cecilia has a unique understanding of cultural and linguistic barriers within the Hispanic community.

Krista Burris

Vice President
Partnerships

Krista is a healthcare strategy enthusiast with interests in accountable care, payment reform, engagement strategies, and digital health innovations. Most recently Krista held positions with a Swiss-based digital health start-up where she focused on partnerships and go-to-market strategy for the US, and various roles at Aetna, most recently with their Accountable Care Solutions division. She has a strong focus around lifestyle and wellness promotion to make meaningful change in improving health outcomes. Krista holds a BA from UC Berkeley in Political Economy with a concentration on healthcare costs and resource allocation.

What drives us.

Purpose, Patient–Centered, Partnership, Privacy, and People.

Values

We believe that true care is achieved through meaningful relationships among patients, their caregivers and the healthcare institutions that support them.

Click on a value to learn more.

Purpose

We are driven to transform healthcare for disadvantaged populations and the organizations that serve them. Being nonprofit allows us to focus solely on serving our mission.

Patient-Centered

We believe that the needs of patients must always come first. With CareMessage, we hope to improve the capabilities of the institutions that support them.

Partnership

We believe that true change requires building long term relationships. In this new era of healthcare, we feel excited to collaborate with our partners to help address their diverse challenges.

Privacy

We remain committed to privacy and security of client health data. In addition to complying with the major privacy and security provisions of HIPAA and HITECH, we take several steps to augment the end-to-end security of protected health information.

People

We are fiercely selective about our people. We value that indescribable sense of urgency and purpose above anything else... although we must say that we have an amazingly talented team.
