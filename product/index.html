Technology designed for the underserved

CareMessage is designed to help you make underserved populations healthier. Our team is here to help you get started!

Get Started

From your first message, to evaluating your success, we will be with you every step of the way. Our team will help you define your success metrics and get you on a path to evaluate your outcomes with data. We will work with you to define the workflows best suited for your organization, while minimizing disruption to your existing routines.

When you use CareMessage, you will get access to our team of experts who help craft each message with underserved populations in mind. Template messages are written below a 6th grade reading level and are available in culturally-appropriate Spanish, with more languages to come. Our product is designed to maximize your team’s reach, while giving them back time to provide better care.

 

Data Integration

CareMessage is designed to work with or without an EMR integration. We offer a variety of options to ease the transfer of data including: Web-based File Center, SFTP, API, HL7, and i2i Tracks. Our team will work with you to identify the best way to integrate CareMessage into your existing workflow to help drive your desired outcomes and get you reimbursed when appropriate.

 
  • 1

    Create Patient Profiles

    Your first step will be to create patient profiles on CareMessage, all you need is a communication method (mobile or landline phone number) and a language preference (English or Spanish). Our system can detect if the phone number provided is in fact a mobile or landline number and can help you optimize your messaging to reach patients how they want to be messaged.

  • 2

    Engage Patients

    Contact patients for the first time by welcoming them when their profile is created, or send a mass message when you are ready to launch. Our system allows you to send one-to-one or one-to-many messages. We automatically handle the opt-out process for those patients that decide they no longer wish to receive messages.

    You can schedule messages for your patients on an ongoing basis. From appointment to preventive care reminders, our system can automate ongoing engagement. Our team offers a library of preventive care and health campaign messages that are pre-translated and reviewed by clinicians to meet national clinical guidelines.

  • 3

    Provide Ongoing Education

    Educate patients through one of our health education programs designed to collect data and help your patients improve their disease self-management skills. Our programs are written and reviewed by clinical staff and are designed to address holistic mental and physical health. They address the most common barriers faced by underserved populations. Contact our team for a full list of programs.

  • 4

    Reporting & Follow Up

    Measure the effectiveness of your messages through our various analytics tools. You can learn from your population and optimize your messaging to drive results. You can follow up by using our automated triggers or target specific populations based on their responses. Data on your CareMessage account is always available for download. Our dashboard provides metrics so that you can easily see how patients are interacting with your messages, so you always know how it’s working.
